Mine Sweeper Competition

Do you have what it takes to beat me at minesweeper the logic game from the 90's. Find a website that has minesweeper and beat expert mode. Upload your names with a screen shot of your time and let's see who is the fastest.

Instructions

If you have never played before here are the rules. Start off by randomly clicking until an area clears. If you hit a mine hear bad luck just restart. The numbers tell you how many mines that number is touching. There is a maximum of 8 different locations it can be touching. They are: left, right, up, down, up and right, up and left, down and right, down and left. Once you deduce where a mine is from this information right click on it. Once all 100 mines are found you win. Record your time and see how you did against your class mates.
